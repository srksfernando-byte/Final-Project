Ryeoun — On Eun-gyeol

Role: Main protagonist and a CODA (Child of Deaf Adults). Gifted with exceptional hearing and musical talent, Eun-gyeol is a high school student who leads a dual life: a model student by day and a talented guitarist by night. He is the only hearing member of his family, navigating both school life and the music scene. A mysterious incident sends him back in time to 1995, where he discovers new experiences and challenges.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Weak Hero Class 2, 18 Again, The Secret Romantic Guesthouse

Choi Hyun-wook — Ha Yi-chan

Role: The younger, high-school version of Eun-gyeol’s father in 1995. Yi-chan is hearing, energetic, and passionate, obsessed with a girl he calls the "cello goddess," Choi Se-kyung. He is the leader of the band “Watermelon Sugar” and is determined to form the band to win her heart. His energy and enthusiasm inspire the other band members.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Twenty-Five Twenty-One, Weak Hero Class 1

Shin Eun-soo — Yoon Cheong-ah

Role: The younger version of Eun-gyeol’s mother in 1995. Hearing-impaired and introverted due to a difficult stepfamily dynamic, Cheong-ah communicates primarily through notes or sign language. She has a deep love for art and music despite her hearing limitations, and she gradually finds comfort and connection through her interactions with the band and Yi-chan.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Light Shop, Summer Strike, Love Untangled

Seol In-ah — Choi Se-kyung

Role: Known as the "cello goddess," Choi Se-kyung is a talented and mysterious student at the high school in 1995. She initially appears cold and standoffish due to a complex family situation, but through her connection with Eun-gyeol and the band, she gradually opens up. She eventually mentors the band in music theory and performance, sharing her expertise and inspiring them.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Business Proposal, Mr. Queen, Oasis

Choi Won-young — Ha Yi-chan (Adult)

Role: Adult Ha Yi-chan, father of Eun-gyeol and Eun-ho in the 2023 timeline. A high school music teacher who encourages his children while balancing responsibility and care. Reflective and supportive, he guides his sons through challenges and inspires them with his own musical background.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Family by Choice, Sky Castle, Hwarang

Seo Young-hee — Yoon Cheong-ah (Adult)

Role: Adult Yoon Cheong-ah, mother of Eun-gyeol and Eun-ho. Wise, gentle, and supportive, she works in art and music education and helps guide the younger generation. She provides stability and encouragement within the family, using her creativity to express and teach musical concepts.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, The Great Queen Seondeok, a thousand Kisses

Bong Jae-hyun — Ha Eun-ho

Role: Eun-gyeol’s younger brother in the 2023 timeline. Deaf, bright, and creative, Eun-ho aspires to be a manhwa (webtoon) artist. He deeply respects and relies on his older brother, who is the only hearing member of the family, serving as a bridge to the outside world.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, High School Return of a Gangster

Chun Ho-jin — Grandpa Viva (Choi Hyun)

Role: Owner of the "Viva Music" store in 2023. Former rock star and mentor to Eun-gyeol, he recognizes the boy’s musical talent and inspires him to pursue his passion. Wise, encouraging, and full of stories from his past, he plays a pivotal role in shaping the younger generation’s musical journey.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, My Golden Life, Once Again

Jung Sang-hoon — Master

Role: Proprietor of the time-slip music shop "La Vida Music". A slightly eccentric and mysterious figure, he guides the time travelers with cryptic advice and sets the rules for their journey, providing crucial insight and mentorship along the way.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Reply 1988, Longing for You

Go Doo-shim — Go Yang-hee

Role: Ha Yi-chan’s grandmother in 1995. A strong and sometimes stern matriarch who runs a local boarding house. She loves her grandson deeply but has high expectations, often clashing with his rebellious, music-focused attitude. Provides guidance and support throughout his formative years.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, The Atypical Family, When The Camellia Blooms

Ahn Do-gyu — Oh Ma-joo

Role: Manager of “Watermelon Sugar”, best friend of Ha Yi-chan, oversees the band’s activities, keeps the members organized, and supports them in performances.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Duty After School, Love Revolution

Yoon Jae-chan — Kang Hyun-yul

Role: Genius bassist of “Watermelon Sugar”. Highly skilled and reliable, Kang Hyun-yul is known for his musical intuition and ability to lead the band’s rhythm. He brings depth and creativity to the group, inspiring other members with his dedication to music. Calm and thoughtful, he often balances the energy of more impulsive members.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Such a Close Traitor

Lee Soo-chan — Noh Se-beom

Role: Keyboardist of “Watermelon Sugar”. Noh Se-beom is classically trained and brings technical skill and refinement to the band’s music. Known for his precision and creativity, he adds depth to the band’s sound and often contributes new musical ideas. Calm and disciplined, he balances the band’s energetic dynamics with thoughtful arrangements.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon

Lee Ha-min — Lee Si-guk

Role: Drummer of “Watermelon Sugar”. Known for his fiery temper and energetic style, Lee Si-guk brings intensity and excitement to the band’s performances. His dynamic drumming drives the group’s rhythm, and his passion often motivates the other members to perform at their best. Despite his short temper, he deeply cares about the band’s success.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon

Kim Joo-ryeong — Lim Ji-mi

Role: Chairman of Seowon Arts High School Foundation, stepmother of Yoon Cheong-ah, mother of Yoon Joo-yeop and Lee Ji-mi. Ruthless and abusive, mentally and physically harasses Cheong-ah, manipulates her family and the household to gain Jinseong family assets. Outside, appears kind and well-mannered, but at home she is cruel.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Squid Game, Queen of Tears

Lee Soo-min — Yoon Sang-ah

Role:Jimi Lim’s biological daughter. Villain of the story, bullies Cheong-ah alongside her mother Lim Ji-mi and brother. Indifferent, arrogant, and cruel in youth.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon

Kwon Do-hyung — Yoon Joo-yeop

Role:Lim Ji-mi’s biological son, Yoon Cheong-ah’s stepbrother. Ambitious to become the successor of Jinseong Musical Instruments, but arrogant and repeatedly fails. Participates in bullying Cheong-ah, treats her as almost invisible.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, The Killing Vote, Cinderella Game

Kim Tae-woo — Yoon Geon-hyung

Role: Chairman of Jinseong Musical Instruments, father of Yoon Cheong-ah. A calm and authoritative figure, responsible for the family and the business.

Appears in: Twinkling Watermelon, Mr. Queen, Bossam: Steal the Fate

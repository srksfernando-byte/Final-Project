Main Characters

Ha Eun-gyeol

The only hearing son in a deaf family. A gifted guitarist torn between family duty and his passion for music, he becomes the heart of the band and the story’s emotional anchor.

Ha Yi-chan

A spirited teen and future father, full of energy and dreams. As the band’s vocalist, his charisma and optimism inspire everyone around him.

Yoon Cheong-ah

A talented artist who is hearing-impaired. Quiet yet strong-willed, she communicates through sign language and becomes a symbol of resilience, love, and creativity.

On Eun-yu

A gifted cellist with a cold exterior but hidden warmth. Her journey of self-discovery intertwines with the band, adding depth and emotion to their music and lives.
